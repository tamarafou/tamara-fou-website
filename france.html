Cannes

I was lucky enough to have connections to let me attend the Cannes Film Festival in May 2015. I spent the majority of my time attending screenings of the newest films. However, during my free time, I was able to walk around Cannes a bit. And it is quite the lovely place and I definitely would love to come back to see it without all the festivities going on someday.
