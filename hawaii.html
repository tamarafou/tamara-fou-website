Oahu

Oahu island is home to Hawai'i's capital city Honolulu, which is most known for Waikiki Beach and Ala Moana Mall. But the best parts of Oahu are experienced outside Downtown Honolulu and in the areas of Haleiwa up north and to the west in Kailua.
