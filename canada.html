Vancouver, British Columbia

So, I hate to break it but I don't have many photos from visiting Vancouver, nor have I been anywhere else in Canada. But please bear with me, I'm going to show you some fun stuff to do here!

The Flying Pig

First off, we have The Flying Pig in the Gastown District. I had a really really bomb poutine (basically fries covered in gravy, cheese, and bacon) here.

Granville Market

Next we've got Granville Market, which is a large market by the sea that sells fresh veggies and fruits, dairy, fish, meats, crafts, and has several food vendors.
