About Me

I'm Tamara! I grew up on sushi and ramen in Tokyo, Japan. After stuffing my face with tea, scones, and fish and chips in London for a year, I flew across the pond to New York City, which is where I'm currently at!

I've always been an avid traveller and have a constant yearning to see, hear, eat, and experience new places! Believe it or not, my 2016 goal was to visit a new place--country, city, part of town, anything-- every month! Sounds crazy, but so far so good! You can see how some of my journeys became a reality, here! So here's my 2016 Travels so far (September, 2016)

  • April: Washington, D.C.
