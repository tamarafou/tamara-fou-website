About Me

I'm Tamara! I grew up on sushi and ramen in Tokyo, Japan. After stuffing my face with tea, scones, and fish and chips in London for a year, I flew across the pond to New York City, where I currently stuff my face with $1 pizzas and hot dogs while studying at New York University!


I've always been an avid traveller and have a constant yearning to see, hear, eat, and experience new places!I want to share some of my best adventures and memories in hopes to help you find yours. xx

Believe it or not, my 2016 goal was to visit a new place--country, city, part of town, anything-- every month! Sounds crazy, but so far so good! You can see how some of my journeys became a reality, here!
