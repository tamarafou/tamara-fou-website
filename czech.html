Prague

I visited Prague in November of 2014 as my second destination during my fall break with my friend. I don't know about her, but for me personally, Prague was a bit overrated and not my favourite European city. Perhaps I was underprepared or maybe it was something about the weather that put me off. But nonetheless, I can't speak for everyone, so here are some of the things I did!

Prague Activities

The Old Town Square in central Prague in the evening. Fun fact: NYU Prague is literally located right there!
For all you freeloaders like me, you can actually just walk around the vicinity of Prague Castle for free! This is a view looking up at the Cathedral inside the castle.
Right behind the Franz Kafka museum (for those who are huge fans of Metamorphisis, there is a small landing by the Danube river. It was actually quite pleasant to see flocks of swans hanging out with the view of Prague behind them.
